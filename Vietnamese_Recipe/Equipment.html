Equipment

This section provides a list of the essential equipment you'll need to prepare the delicious Vietnamese recipes featured .

Chicken Pho

  • Large Stock Pot: Ideal for simmering the flavorful broth.
  • Slotted Spoon: For skimming impurities from the broth.
  • Serving Spoons and chopsticks (for noodles and broth): Separate chopsticks for serving noodles and broth in bowls.
  • Bowls: For mixing ingredients, prepping vegetables, and serving.
  • Knives: A sharp chef's knife and a paring knife for chopping and slicing ingredients.
  • Cutting Board:Sturdy cutting board for prepping vegetables and meat.

Egg Drop Crab Soup

  • Large Stock Pot: Ideal for simmering the flavorful broth.
  • Whisk: To achieve smooth and evenly beaten eggs for the egg drop.
  • Strainer: Needed to separate broth from solids after simmering (optional, depending on your preference for a smoother or chunkier soup).
  • Bowls: For mixing ingredients, prepping vegetables, and serving.
  • Knives: A sharp chef's knife and a paring knife for chopping and slicing ingredients.
  • Cutting Board: Sturdy cutting board for prepping vegetables and meat.

Vietnamese Coffee

  • Phin Filter: The essential Vietnamese coffee filter that sits on top of your cup.
  • Coffee Cup: To enjoy your brewed coffee.
  • Spoon (for pressing): The phin filter often comes with a press.
